    Puropse Of Our Project

    We believe that Nepal has alot to offer to the tourism industry. Travelers are only taking priority in the visiting the famous places & don't want to part-take in exploring in unseen part of the Nepal & so are we. As a fellow Nepalese, we must focus on expanding the tourism industry however we can. So, in this project we are trying to explore new places as well as showcase the old places in our website with also giving the visitors facilities of Maps, GPS & online booking so that they can travel freely without any worries. They don't have to Search different websites to gain information. We provide whatever a tourist needs to roam worryless in this foreign land. Travel Dharan is only a small part of our vision, we are trying to cover every places of Nepal. Hope our country will get even a little bit of development with this contribution of ours.
